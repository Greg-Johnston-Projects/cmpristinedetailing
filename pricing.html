Packages & Pricing

Please note that all prices are based on Small to Medium sized vehicles, 4x4, Estates and Van will have an additional charge.

CITRUS WASH £15

Citrus pre-wash applied to car, Car fully rinsed, PH neutral snow foam applied, 3 bucket safe wash with a plush lambswool mitt, arches and alloys cleaned, Final rinse and dried with plush microfibre towel.

UPGRADED CITRUS WASH - £25

All of the above plus the following, Interior hovered (excluding boot), All door shuts wiped down, All plastics cleaned including pedals, dash and centre console, windows cleaned inside and out, Spray wax applied (3 months protection), Tyre dressing applied and finished with a spray of air freshener.

HALF VALET - £35

All of the above plus, Decontamination treatment that includes iron removal from alloys and paint work (note that iron is one of the main reasons rust forms on your vehicle) Tar spots removed from paint work, Boot hovered, All rubbish from car interior including door pockets removed, exterior plastics treated and revived, Vents, Buttons and crevices dusted and wiped down.

FULL VALET - £60

All of the above plus - seats shampooed along with carpets and matts or leathers deep cleaned by hand. Leathers can be conditioned at and extra cost (please ask).

COCKPIT VALET - £45

Seats and carpets hovered and shampooed to remove stains, dirt and grim. Leathers will benefit from a deep clean and condition, all rubbish removed from car, all crevices cleaned and wiped down. To give you value for money this package will include the “Upgraded Citrus Wash” (door cards and roof lining can be added at an extra cost).

POLISH IT AND WAX IT PACKAGE - Small Car £120 Medium Car - £140

Everything included above but this packed will bring your paint work back to life. Car will benefit from a hand polish to revitalise and bring back the shine to your paint work, your car will then be treated to a luxury wax applied by hand to lock in the shine and protect paint from UV damage, Rain and Snow. All glass will be treated with a rain repellent. Exhaust tips will be polished and protected bring your pride and joy back to showroom condition.
